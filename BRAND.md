# Iris Brand

## Position

Iris is the report layer for AI analysis. It helps agents turn summaries, research notes, codebase readings, product judgments, technical explanations, and strategy material into elegant single-page HTML reports.

## Promise

Iris turns AI analysis into a report people want to read.

## Category Boundary

Iris is not a general document design system. It does not compete on resumes, slides, letters, portfolios, landing pages, PDF-first print workflows, or PPTX export. It competes on editorial clarity after an AI system has done analytical work.

## Core Metaphor

An iris is both a flower and the part of the eye that controls light. The brand connects flower, vision, intention, and reporting:

- the flower: organic emergence
- the eye: making analysis visible
- the page: a single composed report
- the report: structured reading through taste

## Personality

- precise
- editorial
- observant
- analytical
- restrained
- memorable

## Voice

Use clear, compact language. Prefer verbs like analyze, render, clarify, shape, reveal, compose, read, inspect, and decide. Avoid generic AI phrasing such as unlock creativity, supercharge, stunning, magical, or effortless.

## Visual Territory

Editorial intelligence:

- warm paper background
- soft black ink
- faded iris blue accent
- strong report grid
- high-contrast analysis title
- key findings visible in the first screen
- thin SVG diagram lines
- refined iris line mark

## Anti-Patterns

- generic document templates
- Kami-style document suite positioning
- resume, slide, letter, or portfolio promises
- neon AI gradients
- SaaS card stacks
- dashboard chrome
- stock hero sections
- decorative diagrams without evidence value
- literal eye symbolism

## Signature

`reported with Iris`
