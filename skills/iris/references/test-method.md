# Iris Test Method

Test Iris against AI analysis report scenarios, not generic pretty documents.

## Required Case Types

- codebase analysis report
- notes or prose-derived analysis report
- research notes or evidence notebook
- technical architecture explanation
- product or strategy judgment
- workflow or decision report
- Chinese report output

## Quality Checks

- Does the first screen state the report's subject, thesis, and key findings without turning the opening into a metadata form?
- Can a reader understand the structure in 10 seconds?
- Does the lead invite reading rather than behave like a landing-page slogan?
- Are findings scan-friendly and specific?
- Does each section advance claim, evidence, or action?
- Do diagrams clarify relationships that prose alone would hide?
- Do figures appear near the argument they support?
- Is the page premium and editorial rather than an intermediate-note export or dashboard?
- Are assumptions, open decisions or anti-patterns, and meaningful follow-through visible?
- Does the output remain legible on mobile without horizontal overflow?
- Can another agent refine the report by reading the source material, the content method, and the final HTML?
- Is the final artifact a polished HTML report rather than an exposed intermediate format?
